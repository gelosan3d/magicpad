### Inspiration: 
I got my main inspiration from existing streaming decks and macropads such as the Elgado Streakdeck. I really liked the customisability but always though that the buttons were very tacky when pressed and I didn't particularly like the lack of a knob in some models

### BOM:

- 2x SK6812MINI-E LED's 
- 2x EC11 Rotary Encoders
- 8x Cherry MX Footprint Switches
- 1x 0.91" OLED Panel
- 1x MCP23017 IO Expander
